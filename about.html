About the project

Art Tourism Toronto

Art Tourism Toronto is a digital guide designed to help people explore museums, galleries, and exhibitions across the city through an interactive and visually engaging experience.

Purpose

This project aims to make Toronto’s art scene easier to discover by bringing together cultural destinations, temporary exhibitions, and location-based browsing in one place.

Audience

The site is intended for locals, tourists, students, and anyone interested in experiencing art in Toronto, whether they are planning a full museum day or looking for a single exhibition nearby.

What the site includes

Interactive map

A map-based interface helps users see where museums and exhibitions are located across Toronto.

Search and filters

Users can browse by type, cost, and keyword to quickly narrow down places of interest.

Exhibition highlights

Featured exhibition cards bring attention to current and upcoming cultural events.

Future expansion

The project can grow to include routes, neighborhood guides, event detail pages, and peer recommendations.

Why Toronto

Toronto offers a rich cultural landscape shaped by major institutions, smaller galleries, contemporary art spaces, and citywide festivals. The project highlights the diversity of artistic experiences available across different neighborhoods and makes them more accessible through design.

Project status

This website is currently a prototype in development. It demonstrates the visual system, information structure, and interactive map functionality, while leaving room for future additions such as expanded listings, route planning, and deeper event pages.
